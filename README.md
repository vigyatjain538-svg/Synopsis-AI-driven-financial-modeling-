# 🤖 AI-Driven Financial Modelling & Corporate Performance Analysis

## 📌 Overview
This project explores the application of Artificial Intelligence (AI) in financial modelling to improve forecasting accuracy, valuation reliability, and strategic decision-making. The study analyzes how strategic, market, and technological factors influence corporate financial performance and the effectiveness of AI-based financial models.

---

## 🎯 Objective
- Examine factors influencing financial modelling accuracy and reliability  
- Evaluate the role of AI in improving forecasting and valuation  
- Analyze the impact of qualitative and quantitative variables on financial performance  
- Provide insights for AI-driven financial decision-making

## ⚙️ Methodology

### 1️⃣ Data Collection
- Secondary data from annual reports, financial statements, and investor presentations  
- Time period: Last 3 financial years  

### 2️⃣ Variables Considered
**Quantitative Factors:**  
- Revenue, Operating Income, Cash Flow, CAPEX, EPS, Debt-to-Equity  

**Qualitative Factors:**  
- Market competition, regulatory environment, technology adoption, business strategy, data quality :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}  

### 3️⃣ Analytical Approach
- Correlation Analysis using SPSS  
- Evaluation of relationships between influencing factors and financial performance (ROA, ROE)  
- Interpretation of financial modelling effectiveness  

---

## 📈 Key Insights
- Financial modelling accuracy is influenced by both **quantitative and qualitative factors**  
- Companies with strong **data systems and technology adoption** show better financial performance  
- AI-driven models improve:  
  - Forecasting accuracy  
  - Risk assessment  
  - Decision-making efficiency
